About PlayOn

PlayOn is proudly developed by a small but ambitious team at Aerotyne Industries, headquartered in the finest garage in all of suburban America (yes, it's the developer's mom's garage). With a commitment to revolutionizing portable gaming, Aerotyne has spent years refining the PlayOn console between dinner breaks and late-night coding sessions.

Aerotyne Industries may sound familiar, and it should be! We're the same forward-thinking company that once tried to sell miniature helicopters to large corporations (no, really). While that venture didn't exactly take off, we’ve shifted our focus to the gaming industry with PlayOn, where we're confident we can make a splash — at least, after we finish cleaning the garage.

We're currently raising funds to bring PlayOn to the masses. If you're ready to support the future of portable gaming, head over to our Indiegogo page and join us on this thrilling journey. Your pledge will go towards vital resources like better Wi-Fi for the garage and maybe even some pizza for the development team.
